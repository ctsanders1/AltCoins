altcoins.cc

the opensource portal to alternative cryptocurrencies

preamble

bitcoin is the first cryptocurrency of the world. more recently many alternatives have emerged. we propose an open sourcecode project as a portal.
contribute with pull requests. the project is GPL'ed and can always be forked for competing practises to evolve.
